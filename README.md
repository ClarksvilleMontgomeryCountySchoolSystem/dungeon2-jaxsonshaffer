# Dungeon Art

Bring your dungeon to life with ASCII art. Clone this assignment with GitHub Desktop and open it in PyCharm.

You may recode your dungeon tasks from scratch or copy from your previous project.

## Requirements
- Complete all five tasks
- The `not` operator must be used exactly twice across all tasks
- Each use of `not` must be part of a conditional statement
- Check your reference sheet for help with ASCII art

## Directions
For each task, create two pieces of ASCII art. Define `good` and `bad` at the top of your code. Print `good` in the success branch and `bad` in the failure branch.
